Biography

Dr. Meghraj Singh Patel.

MBBS | MD (General Medicine) | DM (Neurology)

Dr. Meghraj Singh Patel is a leading Neurologist associated with Siddhantha Red Cross Super Specialty Hospital, Shivaji Nagar, and Nidaan Neurology Clinic, Kasturba Nagar, Bhopal.

He studied MBBS at Mahatma Gandhi Memorial College, Indore; MD General Medicine at Gajra Raja Medical College, Gwalior; and DM Neurology at IPGMER, Kolkata. He is a vastly experienced clinician offering Neurology services including treatment and management of tension headaches, migraines, stroke, vertigo, dementia, Parkinson's Disease, and more.
