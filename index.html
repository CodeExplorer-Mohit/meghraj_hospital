About the Doctor

Dr. Meghraj Singh Patel.

MBBS | MD (General Medicine) | DM (Neurology)

Dr. Meghraj Singh Patel is a leading Neurologist associated with Siddhantha Red Cross Super Specialty Hospital, Shivaji Nagar, and Nidaan Neurology Clinic, Kasturba Nagar, Bhopal, with close to 15 years of clinical experience in the field of Neurology.

He studied MBBS at Mahatma Gandhi Memorial College, Indore; MD General Medicine at Gajra Raja Medical College, Gwalior; and DM Neurology at IPGMER, Kolkata. He is a vastly experienced clinician offering Neurology services including treatment and management of tension headaches, migraines, cluster headaches, stroke, vertigo, dementia, Parkinson's Disease, Alzheimer's Disease, seizures, epilepsy, movement disorders, hemiplegia, quadriplegia, Bell's Palsy, Guillain Barre Syndrome, and more.

Stroke Management

Dementia & Alzheimer's

Movement Disorders

Treatment of Parkinson's Disease, tremors, dystonia and other movement-related neurological conditions.

Vertigo & Dizziness

Accurate diagnosis and treatment of vertigo, dizziness, and balance disorders affecting quality of life.

Paralysis & Hemiplegia

Management of hemiplegia, quadriplegia, Bell's Palsy and Guillain Barre Syndrome with neuro-rehabilitation.

Spine & Nerve Disorders

Diagnosis and management of spinal cord diseases, peripheral neuropathy and nerve compression syndromes.
